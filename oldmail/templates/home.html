About OldMail

OldMail archives your email from a special folder (or label in Gmail). When you label something with the label @oldmail, those emails are regularly collected to be shared with your team.

This is our solution to replacing the Public Folders we used to have in MS Exchange.

This project was created by Eloy, Jenny, and JMO for the 2012 Djangodash. We work together for Schipul. We build Tendenci, an open source CMS for non-profits built on Django. Learn more.

Share

Let Mike in accounting or Sharon in production know what's going on without having to forward every email from Client ABC.

Simply invite them to join you here at OldMail and start archiving and sharing.

Organize

Once your mail has been collected, you can group those mail's contacts into client groups.

Rename contacts, change clients, and find the archived messages you are looking for quickly and easily.

Search

Whether it's for a specific contact or a specific client, your can quickly find the emails your need.

Search is built in to OldMail to make sure you get the right info at the right moment.

Anywhere

OldMail works with your current email application. You can send mail to OldMail from your tablet or mobile device.

You can also view OldMail archives from those devices in a mobile-ready interface.
